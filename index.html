Why is it important to save the environment


Well, it is important to save the environment because we are sourrounded by the environment
and we get our house, food and air form it. The word environment originates form the french word Environ which means everything that sourroundes us.

Let us take a look at some ways to save the environment.

  1. We should not throw plastic on the road,since the road is not our dustbin
  2. We should not practice deforestation and insted practis aforestation or reforestation.
  3. We should not pollute the air. water or land and should keep it clean.
  4. We should try to use enviroment friendly things like jutebags and biodegradable plastics.
